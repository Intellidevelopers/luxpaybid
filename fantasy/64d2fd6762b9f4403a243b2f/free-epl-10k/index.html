Team Battle
This is a contest between TWO futbol teams
6 Positions are available for your lineup
Captain (1) and Flex (5)
You can select any player for the FLEX or CPT position
Your CPT earns 1.5 times their fantasy points (loss or draw)
Your CPT earns 2.0 times their fantasy points if their team wins

Each player's salary will be deducted from your player budget of N50,000
Stay within your N50,000 budget to complete your lineup

Requirements:
You must select at least ONE (1) player from BOTH teams
Each user can set up to multiple lineups for each contest (if indicated)
Top "lineups" win a cash prize
This contest usually has a minimum entry for it to run
This contest also has a maximum entry for the prize pool

Once the minimum requirements are met
[current entries]/[maximum entries] e.g. 6/12
